Wet Areas in Owasco Lake Watershed – by month
Some months are wetter than others. We modeled wet areas in Owasco Lake Watershed using weather data from the past ten years. The wet areas highlighted below are expected to generate the most runoff – so they are most sensitive to contaminants that travel in surface water. Chose a month to see the extent of wet areas expected in Owasco Lake Watershed for that month.

Red – most likely to generate runoff after rain or snow melt.

Orange– possible to generate runoff after a large event.

Yellow– Small possibility of generating runoff. Needs a very large event or very wet conditions.

Areas not highlighted – unlikely to generate runoff.
